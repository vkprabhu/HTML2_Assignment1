Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of the favourite foods. Here is a receipe for diliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix cream and egg in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
